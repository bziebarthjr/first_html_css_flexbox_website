Airline Flight Information


Airline Flight Dashboard is one of the projects for the Udacity Business Analysts Nanodegree.


This dashboard allows Airline management to answer several questions; "How many flights were cancelled by reason?", "Which day of the week has the highest percentage of cancellations?", and "Which month has the highest percentage of cancelled flights?".


The airlines can filter this dashboard by the origin airport. This allows them to look at which airports are causing the most cancellations.

Airline Flight Dashboard


This dashboard is part of the set that were created for the Udacity Business Analyst Nanodegree program.


It is targeted to help airline management answer several questions; “Which month had the most flights?”, “What is the trend in booked flights by month?”, “Which quarter had the most flights?”, “Which day of the week has the most flights?”, “What are the top 10 airports flights leave from?”, “What are the top 10 airports with arriving flights?”


This can be filtered by airline. The answers to these questions allows management to see which airports have decreasing flights booked and which ones are increasing and what months have the most flights. These are all important questions to answer for staffing purposes.
